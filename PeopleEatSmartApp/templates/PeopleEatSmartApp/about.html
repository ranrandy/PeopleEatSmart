What is PeopleEatSmart?

A website which allows people to first choose from nutrition goals, then get ingredients and recipes which fit their goals. Additionally, people can also sign up to be users of the website. Thus, they can enter their personal info then get more personalized search results. Also, users can help improve the website by rating existing recipes and creating new recipes.

How to Use PeopleEatSmart?

If people don’t sign up, they can find [Select … from ...] specific ingredients and recipes based on the nutrition goal they set up before searching on this website. For example, people can select “want” carbohydrates and carotene while “don’t want” fat and cholesterol. Then after they click on “search”, the website will return a list of ingredients and/or recipes which suffice their “nutrition goal”.
